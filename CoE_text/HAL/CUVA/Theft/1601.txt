1) Criminal groups target businesses in Northridge, causing break-ins at establishments like a barbershop and liquor store.  
2) These incidents lead to damage and theft within the affected businesses.  
3) The crimes prompt KTLA 5 to create a news broadcast covering these events.  
4) The broadcast includes surveillance footage showing the criminal activities.  
5) It also features interviews with business owners who were impacted by the crimes.  
6) This media coverage results in discussions about the broader community impact and provides updates on the ongoing investigation.
