1) Individuals breaking into a recycling center by crawling under a 7,000-volt electric fence causes them to gain unauthorized access to the facility.  
2) This unauthorized access leads to the theft of tools and scrap metal from the recycling center.  
3) The stolen items are loaded onto a truck, which results in the haul of valuable materials like aluminum, copper, and brass.  
4) The repeated thefts over three weeks lead to significant financial gain for the individuals involved, with one suspect, Michael Dean White, pocketing $178,000 over eight months.  
5) The criminal activity is captured on surveillance footage, which results in police intervention and the arrest of Michael Dean White.
