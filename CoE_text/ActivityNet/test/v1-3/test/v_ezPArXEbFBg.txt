1) The video begins with the preparation of hair products and tools, which is the initial step in the process.
2) The stylist then applies a product to the woman's hair, causing the hair to be more manageable and ready for styling.
3) Next, the hair is sectioned, which allows for an organized and systematic approach to braiding.
4) After sectioning, the stylist attaches curly twists (extensions) to the natural hair, providing the material needed for creating the box braid style.
5) The stylist then proceeds to braid the extensions into the woman's natural hair, transforming the hair into the desired box braid style.
6) Finally, the ends of the braids are trimmed, and a product is applied to seal them, resulting in a neat and finished look.
