1) The woman starts by cutting lemons and mint leaves, which is the initial step in preparing the Blue Curacao Lemonade.
2) After cutting, she adds the lemons and mint leaves to a glass jar, setting up the base of the drink.
3) She then pours blue curacao syrup into the jar, adding the distinct color and flavor to the mixture.
4) Following the blue curacao, she adds sugar syrup to sweeten the drink, enhancing its taste.
5) The addition of lemon juice comes next, contributing to the citrusy flavor of the lemonade.
6) Ice cubes are then added to the jar, which will chill the mixture and prepare it for serving.
7) She stirs the mixture with a spoon, causing the ingredients to blend together, creating a uniform and refreshing drink.
8) Finally, she presents the finished drink in a glass filled with ice, garnished with mint leaves and a slice of lemon, completing the preparation and making it ready for consumption.
