1) The group of people decides to go on an adventurous rafting trip down the Franklin River in Tasmania, which causes them to prepare and gather the necessary safety gear.
2) Wearing helmets and life jackets, the rafters board a yellow inflatable raft, which enables them to start their journey through the turbulent waters surrounded by rocky terrain and lush greenery.
3) The fast-moving and turbulent water, creating white water rapids, necessitates that the rafters work together and coordinate their movements to navigate through the challenging conditions.
4) As they paddle through the rapids, the rafters must steer around rocks and obstacles, which requires teamwork and determination, leading to successful navigation of the river.
5) The natural daylight highlights the intensity of the rapids and the excitement of the rafters, enhancing the overall experience of the adventurous rafting trip.
